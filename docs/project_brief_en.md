# USA Car Sales Analysis

## Project Brief

In this project, the analysis focuses on data from the automotive sector. The goal is to learn more about the cars that were sold in the United States in 2019.

The dataset contains a list of cars described by 31 different parameters.

## Variable Description

- `car_id` – car identifier
- `manufacturer` – car manufacturer
- `model_year` – model launch year
- `category` – car type
- `msrp` – manufacturer's suggested retail price (in dollars)
- `mpg` – average fuel consumption in miles per gallon
- `engine` – engine type
- `drivetrain` – drivetrain type
- `passenger_capacity` – maximum number of passengers
- `passenger_doors` – number of doors
- `base_curb_weight` – total vehicle weight
- `wheelbase` – distance between the center of the front and rear axle wheels
- `height` – total car height (in inches)
- `fuel_tank_capacity` – fuel tank capacity (in gallons)
- `net_torque` – optimum torque
- `net_horsepower` – engine power generated at the crankshaft
- `displacement` – engine displacement
- `trans_type` – transmission type
- `brakes_abs` – whether the car is equipped with ABS
- `front_suspension` – type of front suspension
- `rear_suspension` – type of rear suspension
- `traction_control` – whether the car is equipped with traction control
- `parking_aid` – whether the car is equipped with parking aid sensors
- `tire_pressure_monitor` – whether the car is equipped with tire pressure sensors
- `backup_camera` – whether the car is equipped with a backup camera
- `stability_control` – whether the car is equipped with stability control
- `basic_years` – number of warranty years
- `turning_diameter` – turning diameter (in feet)
- `front_tire_width` – width of front tires (in millimeters)
- `front_tire_rim_size` – size of front rims (in inches)

## Main Analytical Questions

1. How many cars were offered in 2019?
2. How many cars were offered by age, defined as the difference between the year of sale and the model year? How many cars offered are older than 10 years?
3. How many cars does each manufacturer sell?
4. Do manufacturers produce more cars with larger engines or smaller engines? Is there a relationship between engine displacement and the model year?
5. What is the relationship between average fuel consumption and engine displacement? Which car has the largest engine displacement? What is its average fuel consumption? Which car is the most economical?
6. Does the price of a car depend on its horsepower? What horsepower does the most powerful offered car have?
7. Is there a relationship between the car’s range and its total weight or aerodynamic resistance, which may be related to the height of the car?
